Connect 2 Protect

Saturday, December 5th 2:00-4:30pm AST

The task of protecting oneself online can be daunting and time-consuming.

In the live session workshop participants will learn to perform their own data detoxes. Riffing off of the buzzy language of online DIY self-care, the light-hearted workshop will provide participants with tools to protect their data tracked by social media giants via their digital devices.

Users can access the information discussed in the workshop and collaborative research resources here.

Resources

This workshop hosted by intermedia artist Sage Sidley  and MSVU Art Gallery, strives to give participants the confidence and time to take small steps towards protecting their data online. Participants will be calmly led through step-by-step procedures on their devices to detox their collected location-based data and prevent further personal data from being collected.

In addition to the workshop, participants are invited to consult, revisit, and share a dedicated open-source website designed by Sidley to connect the content of the live session and additional user-based research regarding location-based data collection protection.
