WORKSHOP Saturday, December 5th 2:00-4:30pm AST

Connect 2 Protect

The task of protecting oneself online can be daunting and time-consuming.

Connect to Protect is a LIVE online workshop hosted by intermedia artist Sage Sidley with the MSVU Art Gallery. Riffing off of the buzzy language of online DIY self-care, the light-hearted workshop will provide participants with tools to protect their data tracked by social media giants via their digital devices. Participants will be calmly led through step-by-step procedures on their devices to detox their data and prevent further personal data from being collected.

In addition to the workshop, participants are invited to consult, revisit, and share a dedicated open-source website designed by Sidley to connect the content of the live session and additional user-based research regarding data collection protection.

Users can access the information discussed in the workshop and collaborative research resources here.
